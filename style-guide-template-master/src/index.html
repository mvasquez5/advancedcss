Purpose/Theme

The purpose of this page is to transmit the beauty of the temple gardens through design with css. We want to achieve this purpose through colors and typography design suitable for different screenviews. Part of the idea is to capture the attention of all people who want to know the temple and make a virtual visit to its surroundings. We want to achieve a good first impression with a readable typeface to draw attention to our main theme.
